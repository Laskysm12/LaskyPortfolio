About Me

My name is Sam Lasky and I am a Full Stack web developer with experience in both front end and back end technologies, including JavaScript, HTML, CSS and various CSS frameworks, Handlebars, Express, Node.JS, and React.JS. Additionally, I have experience working with multiple databases (Firebase, MySQL, and MongoDB). I graduated with a B.A. from James Madison University and completed the Full Stack Web Development coding boot camp at UNC Charlotte. Currently, I am seeking a role as a web developer at a company where I can continue to learn, grow, and master the art of coding.

Prior to beginning my career in web development, I lived in Madrid, Spain for two years, where I was employed as a TEFL-certified English teacher, promoted for trips and events for Citylife Madrid (using social media extensively for this purpose), and helped lead trips around Spain and Morocco.
